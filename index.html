USF ECampus

ECampus helps the USF Community meet the needs of working adults, distance learners and other non-traditional students through:

Bachelor of General Studies (BGS)

A degree completion program that provides non-traditional students options for meeting their educational and professional goals.

Osher Reentry Scholarship Program

Provides tuition assistance to undergraduate
students returning to school after a gap
of five or more years in their education.

Graduate Certificates

Offer specific knowledge in a content area to enhance academic and professional experience while earning a graduate-level credential.

Online Learning

USF offers a number of options for
students interested in online learning.
